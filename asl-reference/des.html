des

Signature

TimeSeriesExpr training:Int alpha:Double beta:Double -- TimeSeriesExpr

Summary

Double exponential smoothing. For most use-cases sliding DES should be used instead to ensure a deterministic prediction.

Example

/api/v1/graph?
  q=
    name,requestsPerSecond,:eq,
    :sum,
    :dup,
    input,:legend,
    :swap,
    5,0.1,0.5,:des,
    des,:legend
